Why Melbourne?

Melbourne, the world's most liveable city

The capital of Victoria, Melbourne, is Australia’s largest capital city renowned for its thriving arts, sport, food and coffee scenes and its warm and welcoming multicultural community.

With friendly people, a great lifestyle and endless opportunities, you’ll quickly fall in love with Melbourne.
